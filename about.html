Get your jobs

Job Portal is a web-application, which aids as a bridge for the employers to find most qualified candidates who are eligible for the post and allows job seekers to find information about offered job vacancies. The candidates are drawn from the pool of eligible job seekers and selected who are most qualified for the job vacancy. Online Job portals are most suitable for the task. E-recruitment has become the standard means for employers and job seekers to meet their respective objectives without using Job fairs, University career employment services, Employee referrals, advertising in the newspapers, televisions etc. the traditional methods for recruitment. With the advancement in technology and growth of internet usage the way organizations hire, and candidates search for jobs has developed. Job Seekers can apply for the job using job portal where the employers have described the job post/vacancy along with the responsibilities and qualification required, in order to appeal candidates. Due to the use of Job Portals, all the process including job posting, applications from candidates/job seekers and interviewing process has drastically sped up which ultimately drops the cost of searching/posting jobs compared to the traditional way of advertising.
